To all you moms. To all you youngsters.

For all you young people who are constantly checking your phone, do you ever feel like you don't understand the graphs and charts that can be displayed on weather apps and websites? Well, to avoid that, this site will make sure everything is both appealing to the eye and easy to read and understand.

You young geysers are the bees knees... You are exactly what we need to continue displaying the news in a fashionable way. We need you to give us feedback on how to keep in touch with the current events in the world. And though we are a weather branch, we need to know what is affecting the world's economy and environment.

We are the break between different news segments, and our goal is to be both informational, and entertaining. Therefore, we try to grab the attention of kids that can often be stuck watching the news out of all things to watch.

Our website will be filled with an inside scoop of the latest drama mixed with the most educational weekly weather reports. This is for all you drama junkies out there.

For those who have very young kids, you know they need to be educated with the right sources. We are the ones who can help you with that because our information comes from the most well- known meteorologists out there. You moms and dads out there that need to keep your kids busy, but also want them to be educated, watch the videos on our website.
